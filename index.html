What is The Halo?


The Halo, is a revolutionary wearable device for sports players. It is equipped with a multitude of sensors which allows it to analyze player health and performance in real time.

The halo, is equipped with three primary sensors which are present on all editions of the device, those sensors being:

- Force sensors to measure cranial impacts
- Heart rate monitor
- Blood Oxygen monitor

Using the data from these sensors and our A.I Algorithm we can monitor players head impacts, inform them to see a doctor if it is necessary and keep a detailed database of repeated impacts. Additionally when a team uses the device in training, our platform will inform coaches if a specific drill or exercise is causing higher incidence rate for cranial impacts

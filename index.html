What is The Halo?


The Halo, is a revolutionary wearable device for sports players. It is equipped with a multitude of sensors which allows it to analyze player health and performance in real time.

The halo, is equipped with three primary sensors which are present on all editions of the device, those sensors being:

- Force sensors to measure cranial impacts
- Heart rate monitor
- Blood Oxygen monitor



Using data for player wellbeing


Using the data from the above sensors and our A.I Algorithm we can monitor players head impacts, inform them to see a doctor when it is nessecary and keep a detailed database of previous impacts. Additionaly when a team uses the device within training sessions, our platform will inform coaches if a specefic drill or exercise is causing higher incidence rate for cranial impacts
